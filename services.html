Our Services

We deliver reliable execution with quality materials and trained teams.

Industrial and commercial structures, RCC works, flooring, sheds, foundations, and retrofitting.

Waterproofing

Protection for roofs, basements, terraces, bathrooms, podiums, and tanks using membranes or chemical systems. Major execution at HRRL Refinery, Pachpadra.

  • Injection grouting, crystalline systems

  • Cementitious sprays
  • Firestopping for penetrations
