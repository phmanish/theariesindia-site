About Aries India

Aries India brings together the experience of Siddhi Infra (est. 2012) and KM Construction Company (est. 2013). In 2021, these entities combined to form Aries India, based in Jodhpur, delivering civil construction, waterproofing, and fireproofing services across Rajasthan.

Our team has contributed to projects with JDA and PWD (A‑Class contractor) and collaborated on notable works including IIT Jodhpur and MBR College, Balotra. We emphasize safety, quality materials, and on‑schedule delivery.

Experience

Over a decade across industrial, commercial, and infrastructure works; boundary walls, roads, sheds, foundations, flooring, and rehabilitation.

Quality

We use proven systems and follow manufacturer guidelines for long‑term performance.

Safety

Site planning and supervision with focus on safety compliance and trained workforce.
